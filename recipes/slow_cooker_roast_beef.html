Roast Beef

Ingredients

Excellent slow cooker roast beef with plenty of juices that can be thickened for gravy. Serve with mashed potatoes, peas, and a salad.

  • â…“ cup soy sauce
  • 1 (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix
  • 3 pounds beef chuck roast
  • 2 teaspoons freshly ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Whisk soy sauce and soup mix together in a slow cooker until well combined. Place chuck roast into the cooker; pour in water until all but the top 1/2 inch of roast is covered. Sprinkle ground pepper over top. Cover and cook on Low until meat is tender, about 22 hours.
